so, what's your deal?

Lucas (b.1997) is from lots of places, but mostly NYC and PDX.

These days you can usually find me in Portland caterwauling at karaoke or playing games that involve too many dice.

Trained as a multi-disciplinary theater artist at NYU, I started learning to program during lockdown and quickly fell in love with coding as a creative medium.

My unconventional academic background (for a developer) lead me to draw on a wide variety of artistic styles and inspirations for my web design, prioritizing unique aesthetics and bespoke layouts.

In my spare time I like to create sites that evoke the spirit of play and fun - see my projects page for some examples.

Besides coding, I also:

  • act
  • write music
  • paint miniatures
  • and occasionally write prose, poetry, and tabletop game materials.
